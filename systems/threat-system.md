# Guild Threat Assessment System

The Guild rejects a single linear power ranking. Threat is assessed across six axes, each rated from 0 to 5.

## Axes

### Scale
Physical size, territorial footprint, or distributed biomass.

### Lethality
Direct capacity to kill or incapacitate humans and other organisms.

### Reach
Distance over which the organism can affect weather, water, migration, disease, communication, or infrastructure.

### Persistence
How difficult the organism or its effects are to remove, reverse, or outlast.

### Intelligence
Evidence of planning, adaptation, communication, deception, memory, or symbolic behavior.

### Cascade
Likelihood that intervention will trigger wider ecological or social consequences.

## Operational classes

- **Class I — Observe:** ordinary precautions are sufficient.
- **Class II — Restrict:** controlled access and trained local response required.
- **Class III — Evacuate:** civilian presence is unsustainable during active periods.
- **Class IV — Regional Crisis:** multiple settlements or ecosystems at risk.
- **Class V — Civilizational Hazard:** effects can destabilize states or permanent bioregions.
- **Class Ø — Unclassifiable:** evidence is contradictory, incomplete, or incompatible with the system.

The operational class is not calculated mechanically. It records the Guild's recommended response and therefore includes political judgment.
