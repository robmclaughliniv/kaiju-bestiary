# The Kaiju Bestiary

A living field archive of colossal organisms, lost expeditions, ecological disasters, and the people who study, hunt, survive, worship, and misunderstand them.

This repository is the canonical source of truth for the Kaiju Bestiary universe. It is designed to support illustrated bestiary entries, maps, expedition journals, RPG systems, fiction, a future website, and eventually a premium art book.

## Core principles

- Every kaiju belongs to an ecology, not a random monster list.
- Contradictions are tracked instead of hand-waved.
- Mystery is preserved where certainty would make the world smaller.
- Visual design, lore, systems, and geography must reinforce one another.
- The bestiary is written as an in-world archive assembled by imperfect observers.

## Repository map

- `canon/` — immutable truths, terminology, and continuity
- `world/` — regions, settlements, cultures, and geography
- `guild/` — the organization responsible for field records
- `bestiary/` — numbered creature dossiers
- `ecology/` — food webs, migrations, and interspecies relationships
- `systems/` — threat ratings and game-facing frameworks
- `art/` — visual language and illustration briefs
- `production/` — roadmap and publishing workflow
- `working/` — unresolved ideas and experiments

## Current phase

Phase One builds a single dense region, **The Mourning Reach**, around three established entries:

- Bestiary No.045 — Bloomwraith
- Bestiary No.086 — Venomvine
- Bestiary No.087 — Orchidia

The immediate goal is to make one corner of the world feel ancient, coherent, dangerous, and alive before expanding outward.
