# Canon Timeline

**Calendar:** Archive Era (AE)  
**Canon status:** Established framework; dates may gain further detail

## Foundational events

- **AE 217:** The Natural Phenomena Preservation Society receives its founding charter.
- **AE 469:** First documented deep expedition under Guild authority.
- **AE 812:** Stratosphere Station becomes operational.
- **AE 1045:** First successful sustained aerial observation of a macro-organism.
- **AE 1503:** Thalassion is verified after a fog-bound landmass vanishes beneath an expedition camp. Bestiary No.086 is opened.
- **AE 1772:** Vespera receives its first verified visual record. Bestiary No.045 is opened.
- **AE 2147:** Gravorax is formally documented during the Xenor fault migration. It is assigned No.001 when the Guild reorganizes the archive around foundational phenomena rather than discovery order.
- **AE 2179:** A full Vespera song sequence correlates with tectonic quiet across a 2,000 km region.
- **AE 2189:** Thalassion reappears near the Southern Reach. The sudden descent destroys three Guild vessels.
- **AE 2287:** Vespera full-song event. Shared impossible maps are produced by isolated witnesses. The classification standard is revised.
- **AE 2317:** Okisendra is verified in the Slaugh Mire after eleven minutes of exposure corrodes expedition metalwork. Bestiary No.087 is opened.
- **AE 2396:** Latest authenticated marginal note currently represented in the opening Guild Archive spread.

## Numbering doctrine

Bestiary numbers are not strictly chronological. Early records were renumbered during several archive reforms. Some numbers were reserved for disputed records, sealed dossiers, regional traditions, or entities known only from evidence. Therefore No.001 may be documented later than No.086.

This is intentional and allows the index itself to carry history, censorship, uncertainty, and institutional politics.

## Dating rule

Artwork may display dates that differ from written canon because generated text is not automatically authoritative. A date becomes canon only when recorded in this file or an established dossier.
