# The Guild of Colossal Natural History

## Public mandate

The Guild catalogs, studies, predicts, and when necessary contains organisms whose scale or influence exceeds the capacity of local authorities.

## Actual function

The Guild is simultaneously a scientific institution, expeditionary service, insurer, military contractor, archive, diplomatic body, and political actor. Those roles often conflict.

## Core divisions

### Survey Corps
Maps ranges, tracks migrations, and maintains field stations.

### Anatomical Office
Studies remains, molts, secretions, parasites, and recovered tissue.

### Ecological Bureau
Models food webs, bloom cycles, climate effects, and cascading risks.

### Hazard Office
Issues public warnings, evacuation guidance, and threat classifications.

### Archive of First Accounts
Preserves testimony, field journals, local oral histories, and disputed records.

### Quiet Commission
A formally nonexistent body associated with sealed dossiers, erased expeditions, and Bestiary No.001.

## Field ranks

- Probationary Recorder
- Field Recorder
- Naturalist
- Senior Naturalist
- Expedition Warden
- Regional Curator
- High Archivist

Rank does not perfectly correspond to combat authority. A local Warden may command an evacuation while a renowned Curator remains an observer.

## Institutional flaw

The Guild believes knowledge creates control. The Mourning Reach repeatedly demonstrates that knowledge can instead reveal how little control was ever possible.
