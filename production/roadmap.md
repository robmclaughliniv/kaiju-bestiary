## Definition of done for a creature

A creature is not finished when it has a name and image. It is finished when it has:

- a distinct silhouette
- biological logic or a clearly intentional mystery
- an ecological role
- human consequences
- threat assessment
- field guidance
- at least two canon connections
- a complete art brief
- no unresolved contradiction left unrecorded
